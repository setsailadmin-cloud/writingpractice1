Understanding the Assigned Topic

Practice 1

Break each prompt into the Subject (what it's about) and Directions (what to do).

Prompt 1

Describe the change in citizens’ attitudes toward the federal government in the last decade. Explain what you believe to be the causes of this change. Finally, assess the impact of this attitude on the power of the government.

Prompt 2

In Alice Walker’s novel The Color Purple, does Celie have control over her destiny? Explain your answer.

Prompt 3

Describe in detail the current definition of a planet. How does it differ from the definitions of stars and asteroids? If size becomes a defining characteristic of a planet, how will that change the solar system as we know it today?

Practice 2

Reread the three essay topics from Practice 1. Based on the direction words, briefly summarize how you would approach each essay. Do not use the specific direction words from the prompt in your answers.
